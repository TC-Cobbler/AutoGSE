AutoGSE bundles third-party tooling that it does not author:

  - Goldberg Steam Emulator (gse_fork by alex47exe)
    https://github.com/alex47exe/gse_fork
    Used to generate per-game Achievement/config data and to provide the
    emulator DLLs deployed into game folders.

Please review that project's own licensing terms before redistributing
AutoGSE publicly. This installer does not modify or relicense that tooling
in any way; it is bundled as-is under the "gen_emu_cfg" folder.
